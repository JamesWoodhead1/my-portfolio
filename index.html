My background

Early in life I had some major surgery which inspired me to look into why I had had to go for surgery. This in turn led to me develop a keen interest in science, which eventually led to me going to university to study Cell Biology.
After university I was inspired by the NHS and trying to help people, as it was the NHS that had set me off on my journey in the first place. This led to a job at Salford Royal Foundation Trust, working in microbiology processing all of the general publics most disgusting samples.
I've always been interested in computers, especially taking them apart and putting them back together. Having built my first PC, I decided the next step would be to learn how to speak to computers.
Thats when I decided to learn how to code!
